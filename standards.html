    Consulting Regulatory Requirements AI-enabled Medical Devices

Standards Development is a critical part of supporting industry and innovation.

This work is supported by DkIT.

My work in this area is focused on the development of requirements for AI-enabled Medical Devices within IEC TC62 and CEN-CENELEC which is focused on Europe. Below is a list of the standards I am involved in developing:

IEC TC62/ MT49 IEC 62304

Software Development Life Cycle

Artificial Intelligence requirements are coming in Edition 2 of IEC 62304. This card will be updated with the latest information on the development of these requirements and how they can be applied to support improved compliance, safety and trustworthy AI-enabled Medical Devices.
