About Me

I am a Medical Device Quality and Regulatory expert and a PhD research student at DkIT, focusing on Medical Device Regulatory Requirements for Artificial Intelligence-enabled Medical Devices. My background is in AI, Software Engineering and Medical Devices. I am passionate about improving healthcare through design research, and I am dedicated to advancing the field of medical device regulation to ensure patient safety and innovation.

Research Publications

My primary research focuses on Change Management within Medical Device industry. Specifically focusing on delivering solutions that can be operationalised by industry and supporting improved compliance, safety and trustworthy AI-enabled Medical Devices. However, other research areas include MDR 2017/745 and the AI Act, Trustworthy AI in Healthcare, Agentic AI and more.
